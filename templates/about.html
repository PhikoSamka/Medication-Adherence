About MediPredict

MediPredict is a data-driven application that predicts patient adherence to prescribed treatment plans. By identifying at-risk patients early, healthcare providers can improve outcomes, reduce costs, and optimize care.

Objective

Predict whether patients will adhere to their prescribed treatment plans. Early identification helps:

  • Improve patient outcomes
  • Reduce healthcare costs
  • Optimize resource allocation

Problem Statement

Non-adherence to treatment plans is a persistent and costly challenge in healthcare. Patients failing to follow prescriptions contribute to:

  • Worsened health outcomes
  • Increased healthcare expenditures
  • Unnecessary hospitalizations

How It Works

MediPredict analyzes patient-specific factors such as age, gender, health conditions, lifestyle habits, and intervention history using a trained Random Forest model. The system predicts adherence probability, highlights key risk factors, and provides confidence scores for clinical decision-making.

Dataset Overview

The dataset contains 180,000 observations with patient demographics, medical history, lifestyle factors, and adherence outcomes.

Feature Description
Age Patient age (numerical)
Gender Male or Female
Prescription_period Duration of prescription in days
Health & Lifestyle Indicators Diabetes, Alcoholism, Hypertension, Smoking, Tuberculosis
Sms_Reminder Whether an SMS reminder was sent (1/0)
